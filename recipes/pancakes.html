Pancakes

Description

Fluffy, golden pancakes that are easy to make and perfect with butter and maple syrup for a classic breakfast treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda in a bowl.
  2. Whisk milk, egg, and melted butter together.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until smooth.
  4. Pour batter onto a hot greased skillet.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and brown other side.
