Lasagna

Description

This classic Italian lasagna is layered with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Brown beef with onion and garlic, then stir in marinara sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, ricotta, meat sauce, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and top with Parmesan.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 40 minutes until bubbly.
